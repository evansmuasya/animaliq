About Animal IQ

Animal IQ was founded in 2024 by a group of passionate university students who recognized the need for better animal education in their community.

Our mission is simple: to increase awareness about animal species, their habitats, conservation challenges, and how young people can make a positive impact.

We believe that knowledge is the first step toward change, and we're committed to making animal education accessible, engaging, and actionable for youth everywhere.

Featured Animals

Learn about amazing species and their conservation status

Amur Leopard

Critically Endangered

With fewer than 100 individuals left in the wild, the Amur leopard is one of the world's most endangered big cats.

Hawksbill Sea Turtle

Critically Endangered

These beautiful turtles face threats from habitat loss, pollution, and illegal trade of their shells.

African Elephant

Vulnerable

These intelligent giants play a crucial role in their ecosystems but face poaching and habitat fragmentation.

Monarch Butterfly

Endangered

Famous for their seasonal migration, monarch populations have declined dramatically due to habitat loss.
